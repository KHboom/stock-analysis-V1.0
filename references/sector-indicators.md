# Sector Indicators

Use only the relevant section for the company being analyzed.

## Commercial Aerospace / Satellite Internet

Check:

- Whether the company is in satellite manufacturing, launch vehicles, measurement/control, ground stations, communication terminals, payloads, or related high-value components
- Whether customers include aerospace groups, commercial aerospace companies, operators, military-industry customers, or core integrators
- Whether major contracts, batch orders, or production deliveries are disclosed
- Whether products include RF devices, antennas, connectors, inertial navigation, measurement/control, power supplies, composite materials, or other high-value links
- Whether qualifications, aerospace certifications, or military qualifications exist
- Whether contract liabilities grow with orders
- Whether receivables are excessive and collection cycles are too long
- Whether R&D expense ratio and product gross margin remain stable

High-quality signals: verifiable orders, batch supply, high-quality customers, stable margins, controlled receivables.

Low-quality signals: vague claim of "participating in satellite internet" without revenue, customers, orders, or product models.

## Humanoid Robot / Embodied AI

Check:

- Robot-related revenue share
- Whether the company is in joint modules, reducers, ball screws, lead screws, servo systems, controllers, sensors, machine vision, lightweight materials, or precision manufacturing
- Whether it has entered an OEM or integrator supply chain
- Whether sample validation, batch orders, or mass-production delivery are disclosed
- Whether single-machine value is clear
- Whether capacity expansion matches customers and orders
- Whether robot-business margin is higher than legacy business
- Whether R&D investment is sustained
- Whether customer concentration is high
- Whether the robot business is only a conceptual extension of legacy automation

High-quality signals: identifiable core-component revenue, clear customer validation, visible mass-production schedule, order and margin improvement.

Low-quality signals: only an interactive-platform statement, with no annual-report revenue, no customer, no order, and no product evidence.

## Semiconductor Equipment

Check:

- Equipment type: etching, deposition/thin film, cleaning, inspection, packaging, testing, or other links
- Import-substitution space
- Customer introduction stage
- Acceptance cycle and revenue recognition
- Backlog or orders on hand
- Contract liabilities
- Whether inventory growth corresponds to orders
- Gross margin and net margin
- Core-component self-sufficiency
- R&D expense ratio
- Downstream wafer-fab capital expenditure cycle

High-quality signals: order growth, customer validation, acceptance converting into revenue, stable margin, sustained R&D.

Low-quality signals: order growth but slow acceptance, receivables and inventory accumulation, continuous margin decline.

## AI Compute Infrastructure

Check:

- Whether the company is in servers, optical modules, liquid cooling, power supplies, IDC, network equipment, AI data-center construction, or compute scheduling
- Whether customers are leading internet companies, operators, cloud vendors, or core integrators
- Whether orders are recurring
- Whether the company depends on one customer
- Whether margin is compressed by price competition
- Overseas revenue share and trade risk
- Inventory pressure
- Whether capital expenditure is aggressive
- Whether valuation has already priced in high expectations
- Whether AI demand transmits into net profit and cash flow

High-quality signals: clear orders, high-quality customers, synchronized revenue/profit growth, cash-flow improvement.

Low-quality signals: revenue growth with margin decline, receivable increases, aggressive capex, severely overdrawn valuation.
